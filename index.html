Home / Services

Our Lawn Care Services

Professional residential lawn care in Kokomo, Indiana. Every service delivered with reliability, care, and attention to detail.

🌿

Lawn Mowing

A well-maintained lawn starts with consistent, professional mowing. We mow at the ideal height for your grass type, leaving clean, even cuts that promote healthy root growth and give your yard a sharp, manicured appearance.

Whether you need weekly, bi-weekly, or seasonal service, we work with your schedule and make sure your lawn looks its best all season long.

  • Consistent cut height for healthier turf
  • Clean clippings management
  • Edging along walkways and driveways
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Reliable, punctual service every visit
✂️

Weed Eating & Trimming

Mowing only gets part of the job done. Our string trimming service reaches every corner, fence line, tree base, and border that a mower can't touch — giving your property a fully finished, professional look.

Included with most lawn mowing services, or available as a standalone visit for properties that need targeted attention.

  • Precision trimming along all edges
  • Around fences, trees, and garden beds
  • Curb and sidewalk detailing
  • Blowing debris from hard surfaces
  • Complements mowing for a complete finish
🪨

Mulch Installation

Fresh mulch does more than just look great — it protects your plants, retains moisture, regulates soil temperature, and naturally suppresses weed growth. We deliver and professionally install quality mulch in all your flower beds, landscaping areas, and around trees.

We handle everything from clearing old mulch to installing a fresh, clean layer at the right depth for maximum benefit.

  • Dramatically improves curb appeal
  • Reduces watering needs by retaining moisture
  • Natural weed suppression
  • Protects plant roots from temperature extremes
  • Multiple mulch types available
🍂

Leaf Removal

Left on your lawn, thick layers of leaves can smother grass, promote disease, and create a haven for pests over the winter. Our thorough leaf removal service keeps your lawn healthy heading into the cold months — and looking great in the process.

We rake, blow, and bag all leaves and debris, leaving your property completely clean and ready for the season ahead.

  • Prevents lawn suffocation and disease
  • Complete cleanup — no leaving piles behind
  • Available for one-time or recurring seasonal service
  • Proper disposal or bagging included
  • Fast, efficient, and thorough
🌱

Weed Pulling

Weeds in your flower beds, garden borders, and walkways make even well-maintained lawns look unkempt. Our manual weed pulling service removes unwanted growth by the root, giving your landscape a clean, tidy, professional appearance.

Ideal for seasonal cleanups, new homeowners, or anyone who wants pristine beds without the back-breaking work.

  • Root-removal for longer-lasting results
  • Safe around flowers, plants, and shrubs
  • Flower beds, borders, and walkways
  • Can be paired with mulch installation
  • Attention to detail — every weed addressed

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Morgan Lawn Services in Kokomo, Indiana.

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do you provide free estimates?
Yes — always. We will come to your property, assess your lawn, and provide a detailed quote completely free of charge with no obligation. We believe in transparent pricing, so you'll know exactly what you're paying before any work begins.
How often should my lawn be mowed?
Most lawns benefit from mowing every 1–2 weeks during the growing season (typically April through October in Indiana). During peak growing periods in spring, weekly mowing keeps your lawn looking sharp. In late fall, bi-weekly or monthly may be sufficient. We'll recommend a schedule based on your specific lawn and grass type.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve Kokomo and Howard County, Indiana. We also serve surrounding communities including Greentown, Russiaville, Bunker Hill, and nearby areas. Not sure if you're in our service area? Give us a call or send us a message — we're happy to let you know!
Do I need to be home when you come?
Nope! Most of our customers aren't home when we service their lawn, and that's completely fine. As long as we have access to your yard (gates unlocked, etc.), we can get the job done. We'll let you know when the job is complete.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, check, Venmo, Cash App, and Zelle for your convenience. Payment is typically due upon completion of service. For recurring customers, we can work out a convenient billing schedule. We'll confirm payment details before your first service.
How do I request a quote?
Easy! You can fill out our contact form on the website, call or text us directly, or send us an email. We'll get back to you quickly — usually within a few hours during business hours — to set up your free estimate visit.
What happens if it rains on my scheduled day?
We monitor weather closely. If rain prevents us from mowing safely (wet grass can lead to poor cuts and lawn damage), we'll reach out to reschedule at the earliest opportunity — usually within 1–2 days. We'll always communicate promptly so you know what to expect.
Is Morgan Lawn Services insured?
We are committed to operating professionally and responsibly. Please contact us directly for current insurance information and documentation. We take the care and protection of your property very seriously.
Can I customize which services I want?
Absolutely! We offer individual services or bundled packages. Whether you just need mowing, just need mulch installed, or want a full-service plan that includes mowing, trimming, edging, and seasonal cleanup — we can customize a service plan that fits your property and budget.
Do you offer any discounts for recurring customers?
We value loyal customers and offer competitive pricing for recurring service agreements. When you sign up for regular weekly or bi-weekly service, we can typically offer better rates than one-off visits. Ask about recurring customer pricing when you request your quote!

Still Have a Question?

We're always happy to chat! Reach out and we'll answer any question you have.
